01 — About

A bit about me

I'm a junior pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering with a focus on embedded systems and data engineering — two fields that don't often overlap, but together represent the future of intelligent hardware infrastructure.

My interest in low-level systems started with microcontrollers and grew into a passion for understanding how software interacts with hardware at the deepest level. On the data side, I'm driven by the challenge of building pipelines that turn raw sensor output into meaningful, queryable insight.

Outside of code, I'm taking an Operating Systems course to deepen my understanding of concurrency, memory management, and process scheduling — all of which directly inform the work I care about most.
